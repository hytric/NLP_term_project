\caption{Embedding initialization methods. The tokenizer, corpus, and MLM schedule are fixed across methods.}

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{lXX}
\toprule
Method & Definition & Interpretation \\
\midrule
\method{random} & New rows are sampled from the model initialization distribution, approximately $\mathcal{N}(0, 0.02)$. & Baseline matching the simplest Glot500-style resize behavior. \\
\method{mean} & New lexical rows are set to the global mean of existing source lexical embeddings. & Stable centroid baseline; does not use token surface information. \\
\method{FVT} & A new token surface is retokenized by the source XLM-R tokenizer; its source subtoken embeddings are averaged. & Reuses source subword morphology/orthography; main hypothesis. \\
\method{weighted FVT} & FVT source subtoken embeddings are averaged with surface-length weights. & Tests whether longer source subtokens carry more useful lexical signal. \\
\method{family-aware mean} & Token provenance is mapped to language family; new rows use frequency-weighted family source-token means. & Tests family/typology prior instead of pure surface decomposition. \\
\bottomrule
